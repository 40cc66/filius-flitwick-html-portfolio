The Best Movies According to Professor Filius Flitwick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Chronicles of Narnia

As a series that opens the door to a magical land, it resonates with the hidden worlds within our own, much like the wizarding world.

The Lord of the Rings

For its epic storytelling and the clear distinction between light and darkness, good and evil, which reflects the struggles we've faced in the wizarding world.

Matilda

This film celebrates the power of knowledge and learning, values that I hold dear as an educator.
